# data

The data for out project comes from this [repo](https://github.com/rfordatascience/tidytuesday/blob/main/data/2025/2025-09-09/readme.md) by @brendensm and @jonthegeek. It's the Henley Passport Index data with 13 variables amongst 2 datasets. In the first datatset, country_lists, the variables give information on a country and the type of visa or access needed to enter other countries from there. The variables are "code", "country", "visa_required", "visa_online", "visa_on_arrival", "visa_free_access", and "electronic_travel_authorisation". The variables in the other dataset, rank_by_year, give information on a country and their number and rank of countries passport holders can enter visa free by year. The variables are "code", "country", "region", "rank","visa_free_count", and "year".
